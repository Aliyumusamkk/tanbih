MADARASATUL TAMBIH

BAYANI AKAN WANNAN MANHAJA

Ni Aliyu Musa na kirkiri Wannan manja saboda sauwakawa yan uwa masu fara karatun Addini amataki nafarko. Nashirya tsarin karatun abisa tsari na aji aji harzuwa aji uku kamar yadda zaka gani ashafin farko na wannan manhajar. Kowani aji da matakinda zasuyi, amma darasin huruf da Hadith duk dayane ga dukkan azuzuwan, karatun zamuyishine abisa tsari na vedeo yadda hakan zakaga kamar kana gaban malaminne azahiri, sa'anna harda tsari na karban sautin karatun daga malamin.

Bayan haka akwai Tambayoyi ko Asignment da zaka amsa akowani karshen darasi sabida hakan zai tabbatarda abinda ka koya azuciyarka/ki. Sa'annan zaka/ki iya yin tambaya ko tura sako tahanyar whattsApp, facebook, email dasauransu, idan ka danna icon dake kasan kowain shafi. kokuma ka ziyarci shafin Contact Allah sa mudace.
